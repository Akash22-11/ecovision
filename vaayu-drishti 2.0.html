How it works

Three weak signals, one strong picture

No single source catches a smouldering tyre pile alone. Fused together, they catch it within the hour — and predict where it spreads.

Citizen photos

Geo-tagged smoke/dust pics sent via WhatsApp bot or app — vision model classifies source: burning waste, construction dust, vehicular haze.

SOURCE: WHATSAPP + APP

Low-cost sensors

Solar-powered PM2.5/PM10 nodes on streetlights & e-rickshaw stands — built for dust, heat and monsoon.

SOURCE: CPCB + COMMUNITY NODES

Satellite imagery

ISRO INSAT-3D aerosol layers + Sentinel-5P NO₂/CO columns fill gaps between ground sensors, especially in unmonitored wards.

SOURCE: ISRO / COPERNICUS

Hotspot + 24h forecast

Fusion model flags hidden hotspots and predicts spikes using wind, temperature inversion and local burning patterns.

OUTPUT: ALERT TO ULB CONTROL ROOM
